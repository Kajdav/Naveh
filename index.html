What we use:

Aloe Vera Juice: This is the base ingredient for all of our lotions and creams. When used on the skin, Aloe has the ability to penetrate the deepest body tissues, some seven layers deep. Aloe Vera 'the burn plant' is a multi-healer, owing to its anti-inflammatory, anti-fungal, antibacterial, healing and cooling properties. Aloe Vera contains a rich source of amino acids, vitamins A, C, F, B niacin and traces of Vitamin B12.

  • Acne: Aloe's anti-bacterial properties reduces acne and helps in preventing recurrences. Acne sufferers require special non-oily moisturizers, which Aloe Vera very suitably fulfills as at prevents the eruption of the acne and infections caused by it. It also prevents the acne from spreading.

  • Moisturizer: The Aloe Vera plant boasts high moisture retention capabilities which is useful for combating itching and irritation. It hydrates and restores moisture to extremely dry and thirsty skin. Aloe also helps the skin to retain moisture.

  • Color and Firmness: Aloe Vera can help in reducing and eliminating minor pigmentation problems, and restore natural color. Aloe Vera's active ingredients work to improve the skin's production of natural chemicals. It is ideal for use on nearly all skin types and is especially helpful to those with sensitive skin. Aloe Vera is a natural astringent that tightens and firms the skin, decreasing wrinkling and sagging.

Red Raspberry Seed Oil: Is extraordinarily high in essential fatty acids. 84% of the oil is Omega-3 (30%) and Omega 6 (54%) fatty acids. Raspberry Seed Oil is excellent for sensitive skin and is deeply nourishing, softening, lubricating, emollient, and conditioning and creates a lipid barrier providing protection and moisture retention for the skin. SPF Capabilities: Raspberry Seed Oil contains exceptionally high levels of alpha and gamma tocopherols vitamin E and vitamin A. This provides the skin with signinficant broad spectrum protection from damaging UV-A and UV-B rays. It has shown an SPF of between 28 and 50!! Healing Properties: Red Raspberry Seed oil is a superb anti-oxidant. Its high level of Vitamin E gives it an important role in skin repair and conditioning. Red Raspberry Oil is known especially for its prevention of gingivitis, rashes, eczema and other skin lesions. Its anti-inflammatory properties are greater than those of better known oils. Red Raspberries contain other strong antioxidants such as Vitamin C, quercetin and gallic acid that are used by the body to prevent cancer, heart and circulatory disease and age-related decline.
